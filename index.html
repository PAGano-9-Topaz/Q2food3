Corn-Silog

Step 1: Cook 3 sunny-side-up eggs

Ingredients needed: 3 Eggs and oil

Step 2: Sauté garlic and Onions

Ingredients needed: Garlic, onions, and leftover oil

Step 3: Sauté corned beef - 2 minutes

Step 4: Pour beef broth in pan 3min, then add salt&pepper

Step 5: Arrange everything on the plate - garlic rice,

eggs, and cornedbeef- then serve

1 12 oz. can corned beef‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎

1 medium yellow onion

3 cloves garlic crushed

3 cups garlic fried rice

3 eggs

4 tbsp cooking oil

½ cup beef broth
